Welcome to the OTC Market Experiment

In this experiment, you will participate in an Over-the-Counter (OTC) trading environment. OTC trading is characterized by participants negotiating directly with each other to determine the prices and quantities for trading.

You will be assigned a role as either a Buyer or a Seller. Buyers will make bids to purchase units, while Sellers will make offers to sell their units. Your objective is to maximize your earnings by trading effectively.

Additionally, you will be assigned an information level, which can be either high or low. The information level will determine how much market information you have access to during the trading phase.

  • Each Buyer can negotiate with Sellers to find the best possible price.
  • Each Seller can negotiate with Buyers to maximize their earnings.
  • Try to make profitable trades, but remember that negotiation is key!
